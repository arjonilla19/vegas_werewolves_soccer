Welcome

The Las Vegas Werewolves are a professional indoor soccer team based in Las Vegas, Nevada. The Werewolves compete in the Major Arena Soccer League also known as the MASL. The Werewolves will begin play in the upcoming 2022-2023 Major Arena Soccer League season. The mission of the Las Vegas Werewolves is to bring the fast-paced, exciting game of indoor soccer to the Las Vegas community. The Werewolves will host open practices to the public starting the first Monday and Tuesday of next month. The Werewolves will then play their inaugural game vs the Ontario Fury at the Las Vegas sports complex in Las Vegas, Nevada.
