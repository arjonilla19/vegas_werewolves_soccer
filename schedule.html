During the regular season, the Las Vegas Werewolves will play 24 regular-season games while competing in the western conference. The Las Vegas Werewolves will play a total of 24 regular-season games with the opportunity to play in the Ron Newman Playoffs. The top teams from each conference will advance to the Ron Newman Playoffs where they will compete for the ultimate prize in Major Arena Soccer, the Ron Newman Cup.
