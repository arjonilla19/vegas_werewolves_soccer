Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the Las Vegas Werewolves website is to promote the indoor soccer team called the Las Vegas Werewolves, based in Las Vegas, Nevada. The site will serve to promote the team and provide relevant information regarding the team. Users will be able to see schedules, line-ups, and news regarding the Las Vegas Werewolves team.Users will be able to access the website from mobile devices, and desktop devices in order to make it accessible.

Audience

The audience of our website will consist of Las Vegas residents that are soccer fans or looking for a fun night out or want to support the local community indoor soccer team. This will include families in the local area and is not limited to age. The majority of our audience will be individuals from ages 5-60. Groups that would be interested are families, youth groups, and friend groups that are looking for entertainment or a fun night out.

Content

Home page

The Las Vegas Werewolves are a professional indoor soccer team based in Las Vegas, Nevada. The Werewolves compete in the Major Arena Soccer League also known as the MASL. The Werewolves will begin play in the upcoming 2022-2023 Major Arena Soccer League season. The mission of the Las Vegas Werewolves is to bring the fast-paced, exciting game of indoor soccer to the Las Vegas community. The Werewolves will host open practices to the public starting the first Monday and Tuesday of next month. The Werewolves will then play their inaugural game vs the Ontario Fury at the Las Vegas sports complex in Las Vegas, Nevada. Come out and support your Las Vegas Werewolves at our first home game! Get your Las Vegas Werewolves merchandise now for a low price and reserve your season home tickets now for 15% off. Follow us on Instagram, Twitter, and Facebook to stay updated with all the latest Werewolves news. Before you go to the match, check out the Las Vegas roster and the full schedule for the 2022-2023 MASL season. The Werewolves are committed to giving back to the community of Las Vegas. We will be doing a back-to-school drive beginning at our third home match. Please bring school supplies including pencils, erasers, crayons, glue, and more.
